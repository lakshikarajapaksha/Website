Ambuluwawa 

 It is considered as the fourth botanical garden in Sri Lanka. It is a multi-religious centre where followers of all faiths practiced in Sri Lanka come to practice their religion. It consists of a Buddhist Temple, Hindu Temple, a Church, and a Mosque. You can travel by train to visit Ambuluwawa Tower.

Dabulla 

 This is the most notable cave temple in sri lanka. It has five caves, carved with a drip line to keep the interiors dry. Inside the ceilings of the caves there are colourful religious images as well as those of various gods and goddesses. There are images of the Lord Buddha and bodhisattvas.

 
 
 
hortain plains 

 Horton Plains in Sri Lanka, is the coldest and windiest location in the country. It consists of ecosystems such as Montane evergreen forests, grasslands, marshy lands and aquatic ecosystem.

 
 mandaram nuwara

 Mandaram Nuwara is a small village situated at the foot of Pidurutalagala in the Central Province of Sri Lanka. The town is also known colloquially as the 'Misty City' as it is shadowed by the adjoining mountain ranges and is shrouded in mist for the majority of the day.

 
Moon Plains (Sandathenna) 

 It is a Wide open area with the chances of seeing wild boars, cows, buffaloes, deer and also a total of 105 bird species are recorded in the area of which 16 are endemic to Sri Lanka. At the viewpoint, you will be able to see the peak point of 9 mountains in Sri Lanka on a clear day.
